Fruit

List of Fruits from around the world

Explore our comprehensive fruit compendium, perfect for food enthusiasts, educators seeking classroom materials, conservationists advocating for fruit species, or those prepping for food-themed trivia nights. Discover the vast array of fruits, from commonly known ones like apples and bananas to lesser-known varieties like cascara, pluots, and persimmons, in our extensive A-to-Z list—the most extensive compilation available online.

This resource serves multiple purposes: promoting a plant-based diet for the benefit of animals and the planet, advocating for seasonal and sustainable eating habits, and highlighting the importance of preserving biodiversity by celebrating Earth's diverse and delicious flora.

For an equally comprehensive vegetable catalog from A to Z, explore our collection. Please exercise caution with some fruits that may be inedible raw or resemble non-edible varieties before trying them.

Embark on a flavorful journey, discover new favorites, and perhaps share your newfound fruity delights with friends. Happy exploring!
